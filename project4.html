Classic Apple Pie

Ingredients

  • 2 cups apples, sliced
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • Pie crust

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Mix apples, sugar, and cinnamon.
  3. Fill crust with mixture, cover with top crust.
  4. Bake for 45 minutes.

indian veg pulaw

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • chopped onion
  • tsp cumin seeds
  • bay leaf (optional)
  • tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, beans, potato)
  • Salt to taste
  • cups water
  • 1/2tsp garam masala (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse rice and soak for 15–20 minutes
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds, bay leaf, and chopped onion. Sauté until golden
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook for 1 min
  4. Add vegetables and sauté for 3–4 minutes
  5. Add soaked rice, water, salt, and garam masala
  6. Cover and cook on low flame for 15–20 minutes or until rice is cooked
  7. Fluff with fork and serve hot

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter (softened)
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla essence
  • Optional: 1/4 cup choco chips or nuts

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix butter and sugar until creamy.
  3. Add vanilla, then mix in flour and baking powder.
  4. Add choco chips or nuts if using. Form a dough.
  5. Make small balls, flatten slightly, and place on baking tray.
  6. Bake for 12–15 minutes or until edges are golden.
  7. Cool and enjoy!
